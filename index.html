Bio

Mentored by Barbara Ericson & Mark Guzdial, my research is at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction, specifically, Computing Education and Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence. I am interested in information literacy challenges in the context of online communities.

I'm a PhD Student in the School of Information at the University of Michigan. Before joining Michigan, I received my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science & Sociology at Georgia State University. Mentored by Anu Bourgeois and Mario Kubek I have centered my research in near-peer mentorship interventions and education-centered natural language processing and machine learning use cases. Throughout my undergraduate studies, I spent several summers/semesters doing internships in industry and academia, including Microsoft, RTX, Clemson University, and the non-profit sector.
